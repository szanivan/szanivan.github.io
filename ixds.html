Tech Open Air satellite event - networking night

IXDS Summer Night - 13.07.2017

the Event

Tech Open Air (TOA) is a week-long festival that brings together over 22,000 people from the tech, music, art, design, startup and science worlds. A major part of the festival are the satellite side events and the Summer Night was among those, adding to the TOA mission and aim to create platforms and a unique atmosphere where people can meet, share knowledge and collaborate. Installations and artwork were created by the talented IXDS team members and food, picnic tables and live music were arranged.
