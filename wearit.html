Wear It Website

the Event

Wear It Festival is a two-day festival dedicated to wearable electronics and fashion technology in Berlin. It brings together designers, artists, investors, founders, companies, research and media in the heart of Berlin and therefore bridges between people, ideas and products. Both days presented inspiring national and international speakers, interactive workshops and roundtables. We offered further highlights such as mindblowing performances, an exhibition, meet the makers session, a startup show.
